About E-Stocks

We are a cloud-based trading platform where customers can open an account, deposit cash, and place buy/sell orders, while admins manage market settings and compliance—all with secure authentication and audit trails.

What you can do

  • Register/login (Admin or Customer) with persistent role awareness
  • Deposit funds, place Buy/Sell orders, and view positions
  • Explore the Market page, add/remove tickers, and refresh mock prices
  • Prefill Buy from Market directly into the Portfolio
